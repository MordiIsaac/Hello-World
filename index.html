Hello World

A program to greet the planet

This page is an attempt to greet evryone on earth using our various human languages. We're interested to see how long our list can become

We will start with languages that the Acedu staff could offer without using google

  • English: Hello, World!
  • Yoruba: E n le o, Agbaye!
  • Japanese: Konichiwa Sekai!
  • French: Bonjour' monde!
  • Kinyirwanda: Mwirme' isi!
